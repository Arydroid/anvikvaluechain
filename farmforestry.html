Farm Forestry Plantation and Species Identification

Agrinet Solutions has conducted various studies to evaluate age-wise farm forestry plantations in India, specifically focusing on tree species such as Eucalyptus, Bamboo, Casuarina, and Subabul. These studies aim to assess the incremental plantation cover over an eight-year period (1995 to 2003) in specific regions of Orissa, Chhattisgarh, and Andhra Pradesh. By utilizing digital satellite data, Agrinet Solutions has been able to gather accurate and updated information on forest resources, considering the significant ecological impact of human interventions and the adverse effects of climate change on food production. The compilation of this comprehensive data is a challenging task, given the rich and diverse forests in a country like India.
