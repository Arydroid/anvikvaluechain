Integrated Suppliers Management Tool (DPM360)

Our AI based tool, DPM360 is an AI-powered tool that integrates supplier management from RFQ to ratings. It handles direct and indirect material suppliers, manages RFQs and approvals, and provides end-to-end visibility. It rates suppliers using a balanced scorecard approach.

Transportation Management System

Our innovative IoT-driven transportation management system manages day-to-day performance updates while also enabling the management of drivers' behaviour to minimize accidents and ensure safety and security of cargo. The system provides real-time visibility and better fuel efficiency, driver ratings, and AI-based alerts to give the best visibility of cargo

Warehouse Management System (eWMS360)

We offer a WMS that optimizes warehouse functionality and distribution center management. Our solution manages the entire warehouse operation in real-time mode, automating the entire warehouse management cycle from the point when goods are received at the warehouse until the dispatch of customer orders. The WMS optimizes the put-away, picking, and storage of inventory into designated areas, thus utilizing space most efficiently. It also enables the dispatch policy of organizations based on FIFO, FEFO and many more!

  • WMS optimizes warehouse functionality and distribution center management in real-time
  • The solution automates the entire warehouse management cycle from receiving goods to dispatching customer orders
  • WMS optimizes inventory storage and enables dispatch policies based on FIFO, FEFO, and more

Control Tower

Our Control Tower solution provides a single interface that works tirelessly 24x7 and 365 days a year to ensure inventory is monitored round the clock from supplier to customer. It monitors potential delays, hijack cases in transportation, drivers' performance, and aging inventory. Its AI-driven analytics provide predictive ETAs, predictive damages or inventory aging, and suggest possible ways to control. Our Control Tower is your supply chain control center that gives you control of your inventory, in-house or on-road
